Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Invoice Management System ("we", "our", or "the system") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our invoice management services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Username, password, and email address
  • Invoice Data: Invoice content, including seller information, buyer information, amounts, dates, and invoice numbers
  • Email Account Information: Email addresses and authentication credentials for automatic invoice retrieval (stored securely)
  • Usage Data: System access logs, operation records, and feature usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide invoice recognition and management services
  • Automatically retrieve invoice attachments from emails
  • Generate reports and data analysis
  • Improve system functionality and user experience
  • Send important system notifications
  • Ensure system security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored on secure servers with encryption
  • User data is strictly isolated with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Email passwords are encrypted for storage
  • Automatic data backup to prevent loss

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in system operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all personal information we have collected
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Export your invoice data in common formats
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Invoice data: According to your preferences or legal requirements
  • System logs: 90 days
  • Backup data: 30 days

7. Third-Party Services

Our system may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • AI services for invoice recognition (data transmitted securely)
  • Email service providers (for retrieving invoices)
  • Cloud storage services (for secure data storage)

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze system usage for improvements

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or system notifications.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@invoicemanagement.com
  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/vmxmy/invoice-assistant-v2/issues
